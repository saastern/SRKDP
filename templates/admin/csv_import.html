CSV Format:

Your CSV should have these columns:

username,first_name,last_name,email,password,role,class_name,roll_number,mother_phone,father_phone,subjects

Example:

username,first_name,last_name,email,password,role,class_name,roll_number,mother_phone,father_phone,subjects
john,John,Doe,john@example.com,pass123,student,5A,101,9876543210,9876543211,
jane,Jane,Smith,jane@example.com,pass456,teacher,,,,,Math;Science
mike,Mike,Johnson,mike@example.com,pass789,student,5B,102,9876543212,9876543213,

Notes:

  • For students: Fill class_name, roll_number, mother_phone, father_phone. Leave subjects empty.
  • For teachers: Fill subjects (separate multiple subjects with semicolons). Leave class/phone fields empty.
  • All users will get profiles created automatically based on their role.
